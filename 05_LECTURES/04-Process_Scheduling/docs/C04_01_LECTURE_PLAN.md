# Lecture Plan — Process Scheduling
> Week 4 | by Revolvix

## Objectives
1. Explain the necessity and criteria of scheduling
2. Compare algorithms: FCFS, SJF, RR, Priority, MLFQ
3. Calculate metrics: waiting time, turnaround and response time
4. Analyse trade-offs between algorithms

## Structure (100 min)
- **Part I (30 min)**: Concepts, criteria and metrics
- **Part II (45 min)**: Scheduling algorithms
- **Part III (25 min)**: MLFQ and practical cases
