### Added
- **Anti-plagiarism infrastructure:**
  - `S04_04_personalized_data_generator.py` — generates unique test data per student based on matriculation number

- Documentation enriched with authentic teaching experiences and local references
- British English spelling standardised throughout (organise, colour, centre etc.)

- Oxford comma usage removed throughout
